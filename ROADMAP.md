# Purpose

This roadmap defines how Project 250 will evolve from a single evidence-based historical investigation into a reusable AI-assisted investigation platform.

The roadmap is organized around product maturity, not just features.

Project 250 should become:

1. A working investigation platform.
2. A public case-file archive.
3. A reusable software engine.
4. A collaborative research environment.
5. A platform valuable enough to support partnerships, customers, or acquisition by 2030.

---

# North Star

Build the world’s most trusted AI-assisted platform for provenance, historical investigation, and evidence management.

The Knowles Collection is the proving ground.

Project 250 is the platform.

Parable Labs owns the technology.
