# Platform Identity

Project 250 is an AI-assisted investigation engine.

It helps researchers discover, organize, verify, preserve, and explain evidence.

The platform is intentionally domain-agnostic.

Its first public implementation is **The Knowles Collection**.

Future implementations may investigate:

• Historical collections

• Museums

• Libraries

• Archives

• Government records

• Scientific collections

• Intelligence collections

• Legal evidence

• Corporate knowledge

Every investigation begins the same way:

With a question.

# The Knowles Collection
