# Mission

Project 250 is built around specialized AI and human collaborators working together to investigate, preserve, and explain historical evidence.

Every participant has a defined responsibility.

No single agent owns the investigation.

The platform succeeds through collaboration, transparency, and evidence.

---

# Guiding Principle

The purpose of every agent is simple:

**Strengthen the investigation.**

Not increase engagement.

Not generate content.

Not make assumptions.

Every contribution should improve the quality of the historical record.
