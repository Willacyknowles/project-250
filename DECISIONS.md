# Project 250

## Architectural Decision Log

### Powered by Parable Labs

**Public Flagship:** The Knowles Collection

---

Version: 1.0

Status: Living Document

Purpose:

This document records the major architectural, product, and strategic decisions made throughout the life of Project 250.

Every significant decision should answer four questions:

• Why was the decision made?

• What alternatives were considered?

• Why was this option selected?

• What are the long-term implications?

History applies to software too.

---

# Decision 001

## Project Name

Decision

Internal development will operate under the codename:

**Project 250**

Reason

Commemorates America's 250th anniversary while marking the beginning of the platform's development during July 2026.

Status

Accepted

---

# Decision 002

## Company Structure

Decision

Separate the organization into three layers.

Parable Labs

↓

Project 250

↓

The Knowles Collection

Reason

Keeps technology separate from implementation.

Allows multiple products to share the same platform.

Supports future enterprise customers.

Supports acquisition strategy.

Status

Accepted

---

# Decision 003

## The Knowles Collection

Decision

The Knowles Collection will become the flagship implementation of Project 250.

Reason

The collection provides real investigations against real historical evidence.

Every feature can be validated against authentic research.

Status

Accepted

Knowledge compounds.

Accepted
